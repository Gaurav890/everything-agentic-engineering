# Context rules

- Chat history is not durable state.
- Read the smallest relevant context set.
- Put durable facts in owning artifacts.
- Keep `CURRENT_STATE.md` factual and current.
- Keep `PROGRESS.md` append-only and evidence-backed.
- Keep `HANDOFF.md` concise enough to read in under two minutes.
- Use ADRs for durable architectural decisions.
- Never copy raw chain-of-thought, full transcripts, or noisy logs into project memory.
